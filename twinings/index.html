The Academy humbly requests the honor of your presence for the Eighth Annual Twining Awards Ceremony. This unofficial Computer Science Department tradition, older than Andy and John Savage combined, is an awards ceremony for the senior class to highlight that which is significant in our department (and by significant, we mean nostalgic and/or meaningless).There will be snacks and champagne, as well as anything else you care to bring.

Prepare an acceptance speech for the awards for which you are nominated. Formal attire required. Donations greatly appreciated; this is not a sponsored event. Venmo @AlexaVanHattum or cash to any of the Academy.
